<size=28><b>Game Mechanics</b></size>

Control all territories by expanding your methamphetamine empire before the authorities catch you.

Upgrade laboratories to increase meth production.
Upgrade distribution networks to increase sells on controlled territories.
Upgrade laundering operations to increase the rate at which cash is laundered.

Spendings will use laundered money primarily, then cash.
Spending cash will increase threat : the larger the amount, the higher the risk.

The more territories you control, the faster authorities will take to catch you.

Attack odds are based on relationships with neighbours territories.
The higher the number of controlled neighbours, the better the odds.
In the events of a defeat, all controlled neighbours are at risk of being lost.
